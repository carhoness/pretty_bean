Caprese Bruschetta

Pedro says this is tasty

Ingredients

  • Good thick bread
  • Mozzarella, sliced
  • Good tomatoes, sliced
  • Pesto
  • EVOO
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions

  1. Fry the bread in a teeny bit of oil.
  2. Add a little oil to the pesto if too thick.
  3. Toast the other side of the bread
  4. Put pesto on the bread and then alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella. If the pesto isn't too salty, sprinkle tomatoes with a little salt.
  5. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
